# 🎨 Header Color Scheme Options

## Current: Professional Financial (Navy → Teal)
```css
--header-gradient: linear-gradient(135deg, #0f172a 0%, #1e3a5f 50%, #0d9488 100%);
```
**Vibe**: Professional, trustworthy, financial services (like Bloomberg, Charles Schwab)
**Colors**: Deep navy blue → Mid blue → Teal green

---

---

## How to Change:

1. Choose your favorite option from above
2. Copy the CSS code
3. Replace the `--header-gradient` value in:
   - `client/src/index.css` (line ~17 for light theme)
   - `client/src/index.css` (line ~50 for dark theme)

4. Refresh your browser to see the change!

---

## My Recommendation:

**Option 1 or Option 5** - Both look professional and financial without the "AI vibe"

### Why?
- Not too colorful (avoids AI look)
- Professional and trustworthy
- Common in financial apps
- Good contrast with white text
- Works well in both light and dark themes
